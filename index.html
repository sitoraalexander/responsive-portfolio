About Me

Sitora Alexander is a talented, business-savvy millennial living her American-Dream to the fullest. She is specialized in Telehealth, Value Based Medicine, MACRA, Meaningful Use, HEDIS, Credentialing and Contracting, Merger Acquisitions, Compliance, Quality Assurance and Chronic Care Management with 12 years of experience in successful clinical and non clinical healthcare operations.

While continuing her education at Harvard University, Sitora also works as a Senior Healthcare Consultant, where she and her team provide consultation and assistance to medical practices to manage and grow their revenue, provider credentialing with federal and state insurance companies, telemedicine, quality assurance, quality improvement, healthcare investments, HR, healthcare compliance, reimbursement and forensic analysis. Currently, Sitora serves 16 medical practices with 30,000 (Thirty thousand) active patients.

Sitora also studies Software Development st Columbia Univercities Coding Bootcamp.
